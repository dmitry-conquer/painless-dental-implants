What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts placed in the jaw to act as artificial roots for missing teeth. They provide permanent support for crowns, bridges, or dentures—restoring your bite, smile, and confidence.

Unlike traditional dentures, implants:

Stay fixed

Mimic natural teeth

Support jawbone health

Are long-lasting and easy to maintain
